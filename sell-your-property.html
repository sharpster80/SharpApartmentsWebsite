Thinking About Selling Your Property?

Direct purchases in South Central PA. No realtors, no commissions, no hassle.

A Simpler Way to Sell

If you're ready to move on from your rental property — or any property — we'd like to talk. Sharp Apartments is actively looking to acquire properties in the Waynesboro area and throughout South Central PA.

No realtors, no commissions, no drawn-out process. Just a straightforward conversation between neighbors. We're a local buyer who knows this area and is invested in this community — not an out-of-state investment group looking to flip and walk away.

We're especially interested in multi-family properties, but we'll consider anything. If you have a property and want to explore your options without any pressure or obligation, reach out. The conversation is free.

Why Sell Directly to Us?

Skip the traditional listing process and work directly with a motivated local buyer.

No Realtor Commissions

Deal directly with us and keep more of your sale price. No agents, no listing fees, no 6% off the top.

Any Condition

Local Buyer

What We're Looking For

We'll consider any property, but we have a particular interest in these categories.

Multi-family properties — duplexes, triplexes, and apartment buildings
Single-family homes with rental or conversion potential
Properties in Waynesboro, Greencastle, Chambersburg, and surrounding areas

Properties with deferred maintenance or needing significant work
Occupied rental properties — existing tenants are not a problem
