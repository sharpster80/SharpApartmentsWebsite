Our Properties

Clean, updated, and well-maintained units in the heart of downtown Waynesboro.

A three-story historic building in downtown Waynesboro, PA. Originally built in 1899 as a single-family home, it was converted to apartments in the 1950s and has been thoughtfully updated to provide modern comfort while preserving its character.

Located in the heart of downtown, you'll be steps away from local restaurants, breweries, and shops. Rear parking is included with all applicable units.
